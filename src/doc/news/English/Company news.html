The rational choice of 4. flushing and laser cutting.

When sheet metal processing enterprises have just received new investment projects, they will be faced with such a choice. Should they buy a laser cutting equipment or a CNC punch press? As we all know, in today's metal sheet processing industry, there seem to be ten thousand reasons to push the sheet metal processing enterprises to invest in laser cutting machines. In addition, with the vigorous development of the generation processing business, many enterprises may start to consider the gradual recovery of the outsourcing processing business, and plan to enter the new business field at the same time. If the business just received a large number of large orders, then if you have such a hesitant as a decision maker, what are the most important parameters for investment evaluation? In addition, before the formal signing of the contract, how should you decide that you have made the right decision in this respect?

Take the cost and the production process as the yardstick

In the past two years, there is an idea in the market that is constantly being spread, and its influence is also expanding. "The era of digital stamping has passed. Laser cutting is the future and gospel of metal sheet processing industry." However, in the view of a senior sheet metal engineer, this sentence is obviously too extreme and impetuous.

At any time, a qualified sheet metal processing manufacturer needs to make proper combination and distribution of different processing equipment, so as to make the most lean material flow in the workshop, so as to produce the most stable sheet metal parts at the lowest possible cost. Einstein once had a famous saying: "in this world, the machine that can accomplish the production task in the simplest way is the most perfect machine." Here we can also borrow this sentence, "in this world, the most concise and low-cost way to accomplish the production tasks most quickly and stably is the most perfect process".

Therefore, when an experienced metal processing enterprise managers in the production planning and investment, he will be the cost of electricity, the cost of consumables and maintenance costs to cost listed in the hour, supplemented by the production flexibility and stability considerations, he will in the popular ideas and practical the results of the assessment, make their rational judgment and decision.

Mr. Peter Visser, a senior application consulting expert of MATE precision mould Co., Ltd., has over 25 years of industry experience and has visited over 1000 sheet metal processing enterprises. The biggest cost control item he participated in his career is to help a world-famous manufacturer to choose the appropriate digital punch and laser equipment for different process and processing requirements, thus saving massive processing cost for the enterprise.

In the Mr. Peter Visser occupation career, see too many customers directly to 1 "this consignment to laser processing", but never aware of the different process will produce different effects on the entire manufacturing process, also did not consider the subsequent processing bottleneck caused by improper process. Sometimes, the customer will use the thick plate to process the number of blanking, but the thin plate is processed by laser. In fact, it is a better choice to change the two.

For example, in the field of architectural design and HVAC, we will use massive group holes to implement design or ventilation. However, a lot of customers want to use laser cutting machine to process, which leads to very slow processing speed and high production cost. In order to let the readers have a better understanding of the differences, we give an example: in a 1.5mm thick steel plate processing 1000 12.7mm diameter hole, if the number of punching die holes with a 12 hole processing, so it needs not to use the 1/4 for laser processing. This means that if customers receive a large order, they will have to invest 4~5 laser cutting machines to meet the capacity of 1 units.

Another key point which is extremely important but often overlooked is that the use of digital punching device can effectively avoid the limitation of laser processing in the whole production process.
