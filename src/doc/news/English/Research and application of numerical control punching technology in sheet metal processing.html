Numerical control technology has been widely used in sheet metal machine tools. It solves the problems of high precision, complex shape and large quantity of parts in sheet metal processing. Numerical control sheet metal machine tools include CNC shearing machine, CNC laser cutting machine, CNC punch press, CNC bending machine, welding machine and flame cutting machine. Through studying and analyzing the characteristics of NC punching process, this paper puts forward some matters that should be paid attention to in NC punching program design, and illustrates the correct way to finish the work in detail with corresponding examples.

Characteristics of numerical control punch press

The CNC punching machine is the equipment that automatically punches the sheet material according to the pre compiled processing program.  The ideal machining program should not only ensure the qualified parts that meet the design requirements, but also enable the CNC puncher to be applied reasonably and make its performance fully play.

Sheet blanking is an important process in sheet metal processing. 1 CNC punching machines can replace the processing capacity of the past 3 punches and greatly improve the productivity. The CNC punch press is a widely used machine tool with two kinds of single punch and turret. The CNC punch press has the following characteristics:

(1) full automatic centralized lubrication.

(2) automatic cooling and lubrication of the punch die.

(3) the screen display and automatic resetting of the hydraulic overload protector.

(4) equipped with pneumatic / hydraulic operating variable pressure plate clamp.

(5) an oversize worktable that can fully support large sheet material.

(6) high accuracy, high speed and low noise hydraulic CNC punch press.

(7) a sliding worktable that can be simply replaced by a die and a safe chain.

(8) the polyurethane free ball device to prevent the scratch on the surface of the material.

Numerical control program design The processing of sheet metal parts can not be separated from the CNC punching technology, and the most important part of the numerical control technology is the NC punching programming. The quality of CNC punching program has a very important influence on the use of the CNC punch press, the efficiency of production, the utilization rate of sheet metal and the quality of sheet metal parts.

When NC programming is done, whether manual programming or automatic programming, we need to analyze the processed parts before programming, then draw up the processing plan, make the right and reasonable process technology, and choose the right mold and speed. In the design of numerical control program, we should pay more attention to some process methods of program design. If we neglect some details of the process, sometimes even if the processing program is correct, but due to the unreasonable process of the program, it will not be able to process the qualified parts at all, even more, it will cause the alarm and damage of the machine.

Select the appropriate programming benchmarks

In the processing of CNC punch press, it is based on the origin of the machine tool. In general, it is based on this datum as the standard of programming. The programming datum can be selected at any position of the part or machine tool, but it must have a definite relationship with the positioning datum of the part. In order to ensure the machining accuracy and reduce the amount of calculation in the program design, the programming datum should be chosen as far as possible on the design datum or the process datum. Selecting suitable programming benchmark can greatly reduce numerical calculation in program design, reduce program segment and programming workload, increase readability and readability of programs, and reduce errors in programming.

Part expansion method

The premise of NC punching programming is to expand the parts.  Generally, the general CNC punching machine program is based on the drawing, using the source code, by hand by step by step. This way is basically not a problem for simpler graphics, but for complex graphics, this way is not only time-consuming, but also very easy to make mistakes. For complex parts and high precision parts, the use of commonly used drawing software, such as CAD, SOLIDWORKS, UG etc. the parts and generate the 1:1 DXF file, and then through the CNC punching machine programming software built-in call graph function, as shown in Figure 2, directly transferred to DXF graphics, then shop mode etc. to complete the part programming. The program developed by this method is not only relatively low in error rate, but also with much less time and manpower.

Choose the right mold

One of the most important prerequisites for programming is to choose the right mold and always choose the same size as the hole. When there is no die with the same size as the aperture, the choice is made according to the actual situation. For example, we need to manufacture a square hole with 38mm * 38mm, while the mold library has only 20mm * 20MM, 30mm x 30mm and 40mm * 40MM square mold. The mold we should choose is 20mm mode. Although it is possible to use the square mode of 30mm * 30MM, but as shown in Figure 3, (a) is the form of square die stamping with 20mm * 20MM, (b) is the form of square die stamping with 30mm * 30MM. Obviously, every part of the size of the left flush is consistent with the size of the die, die loss basically unchanged; and the right of every part is washed away a little, so die loss is increasing. Obviously, 4 times also need to be washed, and the square mode of 20mm x 20mm is much more reasonable than the square mode of 30mm x 30mm.

As shown in Figure 4, there is a long waist shaped hole of 14mm * 7mm, do not use to erode the way, but both ends of the circular mold with 7, the middle section adopts the square die 7mm * 7mm, long waist shape hole out not only within this relatively smooth, but also eroded than die loss the way much less. When the mold is laid, it is possible to reduce the length of the hole by using the way of encroaching, which not only helps to reduce the loss of the mold, but also helps to reduce the burr.

If there are many kinds of moulds, there is a very useful way to set up different molds for different materials, different parts and different moulds. This is not only relatively simple, easy, but also very time saving when programming the mold.

Rational selection of the order of die exchange and the path of cutter

In the program design of NC punching machine, we should choose reasonable order of changing mould, and the general principles are: first, circle hole, rear hole, first hole, then big hole, first, middle and then shape.  At the same time, after the selection of a set of moulds, for the sake of shortening the processing time, all the types of holes that need to be processed on this part should be completed. In order to reasonably select the die changing order, we should also select the best tool path for the mould, so as to reduce the empty stroke and improve the production efficiency, and ensure the safe and reliable operation of the machine.
