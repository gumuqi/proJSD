Flush composite flexible production line

The production line adapts to the high intelligentized production requirements. The equipment consists of a punch press and a laser cutting machine, a storehouse system, an upper and lower feed system and a central control system. It can complete a series of automatic production processes, including plate storage, outgoing storage, automatic feeding, punch processing, laser cutting and automatic blanking. It is fast, accurate and reliable, and can customize all kinds of special flexible production lines for customers.

Automatic feeding system for CNC turret punch press
Automatic blanking system for CNC turret punch press

The modular design of the upper and lower cutting mechanism can be combined with our series of turret punch press to facilitate the customer's upgrading needs of the purchased products. Raw material information, sheet processing quantity and other data at a glance, strengthen the management of materials. The whole machine can realize full automatic cycle processing, greatly improving production efficiency and reducing labor intensity. The automatic feeding device is provided with a magnetic layer, a double edge, blowing, detection and other functions, to ensure the reliability of the whole material feeding action, only 18 seconds to complete. The feeding mechanism is driven by the servo motor, and the pneumatic clamp is combined with the active worktable to realize the automatic palletizing work of the blanking plate.
